Complete Collection of Midrash Aggadah Shiurim

Over 238 audio shiurim organized by category, subcategory, and sefer. Browse our comprehensive collection of Jewish learning materials covering various aspects of midrash aggadah.

Ein Yaakov

Comprehensive collection of shiurim on the aggadic portions of the Talmud, compiled by Rabbi Yaakov Ibn Habib. Includes studies from various tractates with detailed explanations and insights.

  • Seder Zeraim - Agricultural laws and blessings
  • Seder Moed - Festivals and Sabbath
  • Seder Nashim - Marriage and family laws
  • Seder Nezikin - Civil and criminal law
  • Seder Kodashim - Temple service and sacrifices
  • Seder Toharot - Ritual purity

Tanach Midrashim

Midrashic interpretations and expansions of biblical texts, offering deeper understanding of the Torah through the lens of our sages.

  • Bereishit - Genesis commentary and midrashim
  • Shemot - Exodus narratives and teachings
  • Vayikra - Leviticus and ritual laws
  • Bamidbar - Numbers and wilderness teachings
  • Devarim - Deuteronomy and Moshe's final words

Weekly Parsha

Weekly Torah portion studies incorporating midrashic insights, connecting the weekly readings with aggadic interpretations and practical lessons.

Classical Midrash Collections

Study of traditional midrashic literature including Tanna Devei Eliyahu, Midrash Rabbah, and other classical sources of Jewish wisdom.
